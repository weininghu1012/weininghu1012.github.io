ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Weining Hu and I am a fourth year undergraduate student major in Computer Science combined with Statistics at the University of British Columbia (UBC). Prior transfer to UBC, I was major in Finance at Hubei University.



I most recently work as a research assistant at Statistics department at UBC under the supervison of Prof. Wu Lang and Prof.Paul Gustafson. The research work I am doing right now focuses on the analysis of clinical trials and longitudinal datasets. Medical data can sometimes be messy and incomplete and therefore I also develop interest in methods dealing with censored data, sparse data and nonparametric methods.

In my spare time, I like to play around with data visualization tools. Thanks to my background in computer science, I have more opportunities to try many different tools and languages to see what combination could present the best visualization effect and maybe reveal the fact that was ignored before.
